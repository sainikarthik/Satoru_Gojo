MYSELF

"Throughout Heaven and Earth, I alone am the honored one."

Satoru Gojo (五ご条じょう悟さとる Gojō Satoru?) is one of the main protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series.The name 'Satoru' (さとる, サトル) is a masculine Japanese given name derived from the Japanese verb meaning "to know" or "understand. He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. Satoru is the pride of the Gojo Family, the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years. He works as a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High and uses his influence to protect and train strong young allies.
